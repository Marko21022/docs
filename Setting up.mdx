# Adding the bot
To add the bot, click <a href="https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1326478918567333958" target="_blank">this link</a> which will take you to Discord
<Frame>
<img src="/images/adding-the-bot.png" style={{ width: "350px", borderRadius: "8px" }}/>
</Frame>
Select your server and the permissions you want to give the bot.
<Info>We suggest you give Sway <b>Administrator</b> permissions in order to minimize risk of it erroring due to lack of permissions</Info>
# Configuring the dashboard
After you've successfully added Sway to your server, you will want to set it up. For configuring the bot, it is necessary to go to <a target="_blank" href="https://swaybot.dev/dashboard">the dashboard</a>.
<Note>You will be asked to log in to the dashboard by verifying your Discord and Roblox accounts</Note>
<Frame caption="Example dashboard preview">
  <img src="/images/dashboard.png" alt="Dashboard" />
</Frame>
Click on the guild you want to configure and you will be brought to <b>your servers dashboard</b>
<Danger>The dashboard is not yet made to support smaller screens so there might be some visual bugs appearing</Danger>
